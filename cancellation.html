Cancellation Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Order Cancellation

You may cancel your order before it has been shipped. Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled.

How to Cancel

To cancel an order, please contact us at hello@fancycardboardstore.com as soon as possible with your order details.

Refunds for Cancelled Orders

If you cancel your order before it ships, we will refund you the full amount to your original method of payment.

Partial Cancellations

If you wish to cancel part of your order, please contact us and we will work with you to accommodate your request where possible.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Cancellation Policy, please contact us at hello@fancycardboardstore.com
