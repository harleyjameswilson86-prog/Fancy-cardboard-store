Shipping Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping methods for customers in Australia:

  • Standard Shipping
  • Express Shipping

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the shipping method selected and the delivery address.

Delivery Timeframes

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days

Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Shipping Policy, please contact us at hello@fancycardboardstore.com
